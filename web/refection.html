Refection

For commercial agriculture and traditional agriculture, the two play different roles in society. The purpose of traditional agriculture is to grow and produce food and its nutrients for human needs. While commercial agriculture is a planting industry based on commercial interests, many agricultural products focusing on interests and economic benefits are not actually necessary for people, including avocados, durians, cherries and so on. The high profits of these economic fruits have led many agricultural companies to plant a large number of fruits in non-native areas, which has led to serious damage to water resources, soil resources and local natural ecosystems.
