Detailed Description

Data Collection

I have done data visualization research on this topic before, and I believe that the excessive consumption of environmental resources by cash crops needs to be understood by consumers, so I collected data on this phenomenon through various ways. I hope to show this phenomenon through data visual modeling and data comparison. I used some technical skills and some visualization websites to show the visual model.

Introduction

As we all know, agriculture is one of the largest and most important economic activities of human beings. The emergence of various agriculture is the reason for the rise and development of human society. Based on the uses of agricultural products, we can divide agriculture into economic agriculture and self-sustaining agriculture. In modern society, people's pursuit of food is not only satisfied with satiety, but also the pursuit of taste, novelty and health. As a result, the planting of economic crops began to expand wildly, and the ecological destruction and ecological resource consumption of many economic crops in the planting process is amazing.
